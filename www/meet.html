Meet Julie & Cody
About Julie Wilkes
After overcoming a congenital heart condition that gave her a short life expectancy, Julie dedicates her life to inspiring others to live with intention and passion. Julie holds Master's Degree with a focus on Exercise Physiology from The Ohio State University, certifications in yoga, group fitness and personal training from a variety of national accredited organizations, and is a Master Trainer and Instructor. Julie is a life coach, an international lecturer and author and has given over 2000 motivational, health and wellness lectures in the past 10 years. Julie is the proud mom of Cody, the Fitness dog, and Sancho, the Fitness cat. Both Cody and Sancho have used the 4 Paws Workout and lost weight, improved strength, gained energy and improved their quality of life!
About Cody, the Fitness Dog - the star of the workout!
Cody, the Fitness Dog, is the inspiration behind the 4 Paws Workout. Cody is Julie's 'furry angel'. Cody is named after a boy who passed away of a terminal illness and helps to raise money to grant wishes for children with life-threatening illnesses by being the poster dog for many fundraising events.
Cody is a long-haired dachschund, and staying true-to-her-breed - is a food lover! She will eat anything! Cody's vet recommended that Julie place Cody on a diet, in order to lose a few pounds and help to prevent future back injuries. Julie developed the 4 Paw Fitness workout so that she and Cody would spend quality time together, while exercising. Cody was able to reach her ideal weight and increased her energy, as a result of the 4 Paws Workout. She now does it 3-4 times a week, to maintain her health and happiness. And, she thinks its a lot of fun!
